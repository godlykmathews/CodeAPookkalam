How to Register on the TinkerHub App

For first-timers, here is how you can register for the event using the TinkerHub app. It's the best way to stay updated and be part of the community!

  1. Download the App: Get the TinkerHub app from the Google Play Store or Apple App Store.
  2. Create Your Account: Sign up with your email or social media account. If you already have an account, just log in.
  3. Find the Event: Navigate to the 'Events' or 'Hubs' section in the app.
  4. Search for Code-a-Pookkalam: Look for the "Code-a-Pookkalam" event and tap on it.
  5. Register: Click the 'Register' or 'Join' button to confirm your participation.
  6. You're All Set! Get ready to code your beautiful Pookkalam and share it with the community.
